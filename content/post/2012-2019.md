> Photography is (a means by which we)...learn to see the ordinary.
>
> ~ David Bailey

> “A photographer is like a cod, which produces a million eggs in order that one may reach maturity.”
?
> ~ George Bernard Shaw

Between work and family, there was not much time for photography in the early 2000s. That began to change in 2012
as our kids entered their senior year in high school and I acquired an [Olympus OM-D EM-5](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Olympus_OM-D_E-M5).
Previously, I had mostly used telephoto zoom lenses for landscape photography, but with the small Olympus and
a couple of fixed length prime lenses I dug up the courage to emulate my heroes (Elliott Erwitt, Ian Berry, Henri 
Cartier-Bresson, etc), and make candid photographs of people on the street.
