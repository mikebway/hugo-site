Another change of employer in late 2022 brought back the possibility of longer transatlantic trips, in particular, one
to Bologna and Florence in 2023. The possibility of AI image generation and/or manipulation became an creative and
ethical conundrum. 

While I appreciate that AI focus correction saved one of my favorite images from 2023, I am
resolved not to use it again. See the blog post, ["Successful Street Photo or AI cheat?"](https://medium.com/@mikebroadway/successful-street-photo-or-ai-cheat-c5253dc91d89)
on Medium for a longer discussion of that one image.
